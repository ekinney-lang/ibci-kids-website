Implantable Brain-Computer Interfaces (iBCI)
for Children with Complex Needs

We are an international collaboration of researchers, clinicians, and engineers working to develop implantable brain-computer interfaces (iBCIs) for children with complex needs. Our goal is to make sure that children who could benefit from iBCIs are not left behind as this technology continues to mature. We believe that children with complex needs deserve the same opportunities to communicate, learn, and play as anyone else, and are working to make this a reality.
